## ðŸ’ª MINDSET REMINDERS

When you get tired (and you will):

1. **Rejection is OK** - Most people will say no. That's normal. 10% say yes = success.

2. **Speed over perfection** - Ship v0.9, get feedback, iterate. Don't wait for perfect.

3. **Talk to users constantly** - Spend 50% of your time talking to teachers. They'll tell you exactly what to build next.

4. **Build for one person deeply** - Don't try to appeal to everyone. Build for one teacher so well that they can't live without it.

5. **Revenue changes everything** - First paying customer will motivate you more than 1,000 free signups.
