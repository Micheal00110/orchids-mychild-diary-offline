## âš ï¸ BIGGEST RISKS & MITIGATION

### Risk 1: Teacher Adoption (Biggest Risk)
**Risk:** Teachers like the app but don't actually use it daily

**Mitigation:**
1. Onboarding calls (1-on-1 with first 50 users)
2. In-app tutorials (reduce time to first diary entry)
3. Habit building (send reminders at 3:30pm - homework time)
4. Gamification (streak counter - "20 days filling homework")

**Metric:** >50% of teachers filling diary at least 2x/week

---

### Risk 2: Parent Engagement (Second Biggest Risk)
**Risk:** Teachers fill diary but parents don't read it

**Mitigation:**
1. Push notifications (SMS for non-active parents)
2. Email digest ("Weekly homework for your child")
3. School integration (school sends your link in newsletter)
4. Gamification (parents get badge for "Work Ensured" 20 times)

**Metric:** >60% of parents signing daily/weekly

---

### Risk 3: Competition (Will Happen)
**Risk:** Larger ed-tech company copies you

**Mitigation:**
1. Move fast (get 10,000 users before they enter)
2. Build network effects (teacher â†’ parent â†’ school)
3. Get school contracts (sticky, hard to leave)
4. Build moat (partnerships, integrations)

**Metric:** 1,000+ paying customers by Month 12 (sticky base)

---

### Risk 4: Unit Economics (Financial Risk)
**Risk:** CAC too high, LTV too low = unsustainable

**Mitigation:**
1. Reduce CAC through organic (don't spend on ads until Month 4)
2. Increase LTV through upsells (free â†’ $6 â†’ school plan)
