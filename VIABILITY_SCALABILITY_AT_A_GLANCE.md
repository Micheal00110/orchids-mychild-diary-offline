## üö® BIGGEST RISKS (Plan For These Now)

### Risk 1: Teacher Adoption
**Problem:** Teachers download but don't use daily

**Solution:** 
- Onboarding calls (1-on-1 with first 50)
- In-app tutorials
- Habit building (send 3:30pm homework reminder)

**Success Metric:** >50% filling diary 2x/week

---

### Risk 2: Parent Engagement
**Problem:** Teachers fill diary but parents don't see it

**Solution:**
- Push notifications (SMS to non-active parents)
- Email digest (weekly homework summary)
- School integration (school recommends)

**Success Metric:** >60% of parents signing daily/weekly

---

### Risk 3: Competition
**Problem:** Large company copies you

**Solution:**
- Move fast (1,000+ users before they enter)
- Get contracts (sticky customer lock-in)
- Build network effects (teachers ‚Üî parents)

**Success Metric:** 1,000+ paying customers by Month 12
